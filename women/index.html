Women in STEM

This section of Skyward She is dedicated to women and girls who are shaping science, technology, engineering and mathematics — past, present and future.

Here you’ll find profiles of inspiring women, personal journeys, and resources designed to encourage girls who are curious, ambitious and ready to build their place in STEM.

Profiles

Women who made — and are making — history in science and engineering.

Stories & journeys

Personal paths, challenges, and lessons from girls starting early in STEM.

Resources

Books, projects, competitions and ideas to explore STEM at any age.
