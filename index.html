About Catarina

Catarina is a 14-year-old aspiring aerospace engineer from Porto Velho, a city in the northern Brazilian state of Rondônia. Born on November 27, she is passionate about physics, aerospace engineering, and the future of space exploration.

She discovered her love for STEM through school projects, research, and science olympiads. Every challenge — from studying flight mechanics to writing about women in aerospace — brings her closer to the future she dreams of building.

Through Skyward She, Catarina hopes to inspire other girls, especially those from places where STEM opportunities feel far away, to believe that curiosity and ambition can take you anywhere — even to the stars.
